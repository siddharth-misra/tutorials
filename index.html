Every tutorial ships with three reading modes.

Chapter-by-chapter

Each tutorial is split into focused chapters. Navigate with the sidebar or the prev/next controls at the top — only the chapter you're on is rendered, so it stays fast and distraction-free.

Switch to single-page mode to see the entire tutorial on one scrollable page — useful for searching across all chapters with Cmd+F, or reading linearly from start to finish without clicking through.

Once you can name them on sight — sliding window, two pointers, fast and slow, BFS/DFS, merge intervals, backtracking — most problems become variations on something you've already solved. This track builds that recognition deliberately.
